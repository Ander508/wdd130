Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to provide a recreation centre where typically a non-motorized water craft floats and navigates down free flowing rivers. We organizes and manages trips which most often include experienced guides. Our business intent is to provide a thrilling and safe experience to individuals and groups for a fee. Our purpose of a water rafting company extends beyond simply providing entertainment; it encompasses elements of education, conservation, economic development, and community engagement.

Audience

Our customers include individuals(young and and mid aged adventurers) and groups with varied interests, backgrounds, and motivations for seeking out outdoor adventure experiences;

  • Who are our target customers?: Our target customers includes but not limited to
    • Adventure Seekers: Individuals who enjoy outdoor activities and are seeking thrilling experiences. They may be adrenaline junkies looking for excitement and new challenges.
    • Tourists: Visitors to the area where the water rafting company operates, whether they are tourists from other countries or domestic travelers looking for unique experiences.
    • Families Families looking for adventurous yet safe activities to enjoy together. Water rafting companies often offer family-friendly trips suitable for various age groups.
    • Outdoor Enthusiasts: People who have a passion for outdoor recreation and enjoy activities such as hiking, camping, and kayaking. Water rafting can be an exciting addition to their list of outdoor adventures.
  • What do our customers want?
    • Thrilling Experience: Customers are often drawn to water rafting for the adrenaline rush and excitement of navigating through rapids and rough waters. They want an experience that challenges them physically and mentally.
    • Scenic Beauty: Many customers appreciate the natural beauty of the river or canyon where the rafting takes place. They seek out experiences that allow them to immerse themselves in stunning landscapes and observe wildlife while enjoying the thrill of rafting.
    • Memorable Moments: Customers want experiences they can cherish and remember fondly. Whether it's capturing photos of their adventure, enjoying camaraderie with fellow rafters, or overcoming personal challenges, they seek moments that create lasting memories.
  • How will our audience usually access my site: Our customers can access our site through the use of Desktops, Laptops, Mobile devices.
